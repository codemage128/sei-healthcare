FAQ

PRODUCT

What is the shelf life of Botanical Affairs products?

Both our finished products and the raw materials have an unopened shelf life of at least 30 months. In general, you can recognize the shelf life of a product by the symbol of the opened cream jar. Everything that has a shelf life of more than 30 months before opening bears this symbol. Next to the jar you will find a number. This number tells you how many months the product will keep after it has been opened for the first time.

If the products smell strange, feel odd on the skin or have an unusual consistency, simply contact us.

How long do I have to use the products?

With regular use in combination with oil and sitz bath, you should feel significant relief of your symptoms within 30 days. The length of time it takes to achieve lasting results varies from person to person. Regular use for three months is often necessary to remain permanently symptom-free.

How to apply the oil?

The oil should be applied to the abdomen and lower back at least three times a day and massaged in lightly.

How to use the sitz bath?

The bath salts should be placed in a bowl or container where you can sit and relax, such as a wash bowl or bidet. The recommended amount of one tablespoon is limited to the use of a sitz bath. The bath salts are not intended for a full bath, as it can lead to further inflammation due to possible bacteria in the water.

How to use the lubricant gel?

The gel can be used like a traditional lubricant during intimate moments with your partner, alone or simply in between to moisturize and relieve local symptoms.

SHIPPING AND RETURNS

Can you really eat the packaging material?

The packaging material is made of pea or maize starch, so you can actually eat it. However, if you don't like it, it's best to dispose of it in the organic waste bin or dissolve it in water to water your flowers. The material makes a fantastic plant fertilizer!

How will my order be shipped?

We ship climate neutrally with DHL Go Green. The emissions generated during transport are neutralized 100% without any additional costs for you as a customer! You can find more information about this under the following link on the page of DHL Go Green.

My parcel is in a post office - and I can't fetch it!

If you are unable to collect your parcel from the post office within the storage period of seven working days, it will be returned to us. As soon as we receive it, we will contact you. We will then be happy to send it to you again! We may ask you for an alternative address where your parcel can be collected in person.

How can I track the shipment of my order?

You can do this simply by clicking on the DHL tracking link, which will be sent to your e-mail address as soon as we have handed over your parcel to DHL. With the shipment tracking you can track the shipment of your order step by step and see the estimated time of delivery.

Returns

You want to return your Botanical Affairs products?

If your products are still sealed, we will gladly refund your money. We ask for your understanding that for hygienic reasons we can only take back unopened items.

How can I return my products?

1 — Pack the unopened items in a shipping box.

2 — Enter your personal data in our returns portal and print out our shipping labels. In the printed document you will also find detailed instructions on what to do.

3 — Attach the DHL returns label to the shipping box so that it is clearly visible.

4 — Take the parcel to a DHL branch or Packstation near you. We will of course pay the postage for the shipment!

5 — After our incoming goods department has checked the seals of the returned items, you will receive a credit note from us in the amount of the original purchase price.
